nebari-apps has been installed.

The apps-operator is now watching for App resources (apps.nebari.dev/v1alpha1)
in namespaces labeled nebari.dev/managed=true.

Launch your first app:

  kubectl label namespace <your-namespace> nebari.dev/managed=true
  kubectl apply -n <your-namespace> -f https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nebari-dev/apps-pack/main/examples/static-inline-app.yaml

Then watch it come up:

  kubectl get apps -n <your-namespace> -w

Once Phase is Running, the app is reachable at the URL in the App status
(https://<subdomain>.{{ include "nebari-apps.appsDomain" . }}) behind
Keycloak SSO (unless access.public is true).

Requires the nebari-operator (NebariApp CRD) to be installed for routing,
TLS, and authentication.
